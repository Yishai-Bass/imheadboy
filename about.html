About ImHeadBoy Merch

Welcome to ImHeadBoy Merch—the official store inspired by the unsung hero of every wedding: the groom's younger brother, who’s so overachieving it’s borderline legendary.

Born from years of sibling rivalry, relentless ambition, and a humble (if not slightly obsessive) desire for recognition, our store celebrates the art of being Head Boy. Why just earn a title when you can wear it on a mug, a cap, or even a pillow?

At ImHeadBoy Merch, we specialize in top-tier gear that shouts, "I’m not just a sibling—I’m the Head Boy’s kin!" Whether you’re here to remind everyone of your inherent superiority or simply to enjoy a touch of familial jest, we’ve got you covered.

Remember: Being Head Boy isn’t just a title—it’s a lifestyle. And maybe a slight obsession.
